Burger Bliss

A responsive restaurant platform showcasing various dishes like burgers, pizza, and cakes. Users can view details, place orders, and enjoy delivery services.

Project Overview

This restaurant project allows users to explore a variety of dishes, including burgers, pizzas, and cakes. Built using HTML and CSS, the platform ensures that the design is responsive and attractive. Users can browse dish details and place orders online, while delivery services are also available. Special attention was given to the visual aspects of the site, ensuring a seamless experience across all devices.

Available Dishes

The platform features a wide variety of dishes including burgers, pizzas, cakes, and more. Each dish has detailed descriptions, including ingredients and pricing. The design highlights the mouth-watering appeal of each dish, offering users an inviting and easy-to-navigate experience. Additionally, customers can filter dishes by category to find their favorites faster.

Order Functionality

The website provides users with a smooth ordering experience, allowing them to place their desired items in the cart and proceed to checkout. All orders are processed securely, and users can choose their preferred method of payment. The design ensures the checkout process is quick and user-friendly, offering a delightful experience from browsing to ordering.

Delivery Service

For added convenience, the platform offers a delivery service for all orders. Customers can enter their delivery address and track the status of their order in real-time. Whether it's a quick burger delivery or a full meal, the system ensures timely service with clear status updates throughout the process.
